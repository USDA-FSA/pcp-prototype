PCP-II Prototype

Component: Growl

Inserted into beginning of DOM, i.e. first element in <body>

<div class="fsa-growl-container">
  <div class="fsa-growl fsa-growl--[variation]">...</div>
</div>

To center it in a modal fashion use...

<div class="fsa-growl-container fsa-growl-container--centered">...</div>

As seen on

  • settings_commodities.html (Click "Add", and then submit form "Save")
  • settings_markets__add-success.html
  • settings_markets__error.html
  • workflow_mapping.html (Click "Save" or "Reset")
